Sakai Mobile

Introducing a better, easier way to navigate Rutgers Sakai.

About

Rutgers Sakai Mobile is an intuitive application designed to make browsing Rutgers' Sakai LMS easier than ever. Rutgers Sakai Mobile takes the best of Sakai's features and makes them at home on mobile. Rutgers Sakai Mobile is NOT affiliated with Rutgers University or the Sakai LMS project and is an entirely third-party solution.

You can contact the developers with concerns or feedback at rutgerssakaiapp@gmail.com
