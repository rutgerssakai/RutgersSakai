Introducing a better, easier way to use Rutgers Sakai on Android and iOS

Sakai, now in your pocket.

Sakai Mobile is designed to make navigating your coursework easier than ever.
We took the best of Sakai's features and made them feel at home on mobile.
Rutgers Sakai Mobile is not affiliated with Rutgers University or the Sakai LMS project and is an entirely third-party solution.

Secure Login

We take data privacy very seriously, and ensure that your credentials never leave the application. Sakai uses cookie-based authentication and login is performed through a WebView over HTTPS, so we as developers never get access to your NetID or password. To learn more, check out our privacy policy.

Classes and Sites

All of your courses are organized in one central landing page in an intuitive tree structure sorted by semester. Gone are the days of accidentally clicking the wrong button and waiting an eternity for Sakai to load before going where you really wanted to go.

Announcements

Your email and daily news are structured as feeds, so why shouldn't your announcements be? View all announcements for all your classes in a single, streamlined feed format. You can also view announcements for just a single course through the main Classes tab.

Resources

Hate how Sakai keeps opening a new page for every folder in the Resources tab?
So did we.

Now you can view course resources in a single screen — just the way it should've been all along. As usual, you may download and view any file type natively.

Gradebook

Whether you want to check if your professor put in a grade recently, or see how you did on an assignment last semester, Sakai Mobile has you covered. View grades for all your classes in a centralized location.

Assignments

Ever needed to log into Sakai just to check one small detail for an assignment? You can view full assignment details, including attachments and instructions, for all of your assignments in Sakai Mobile. Assignments can be submitted directly from the app as well, allowing you to submit documents from Google Drive or the native filesystem with much more ease.
