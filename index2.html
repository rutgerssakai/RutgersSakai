Introducing a better, easier way to use Rutgers Sakai on Android and iOS

Sakai, now in your pocket.

Sakai Mobile is designed to make navigating your coursework easier than ever.
We took the best of Sakai's features and made them feel at home on mobile.
Rutgers Sakai Mobile is not affiliated with Rutgers University or the Sakai LMS project and is an entirely third-party solution.
