About

Rutgers Sakai Mobile is an intuitive application designed to make browsing Rutgers' Sakai LMS easier than ever. Rutgers Sakai Mobile takes the best of Sakai's features and makes them at home on mobile.

You can contact the developers with concerns or feedback at rutgerssakaiapp@gmail.com
